Waiting for Wonderland Trail permit

April 7, 2020

About 20 year ago I climbed Mount Rainier and learned about the 93 mile trail around it's base, the Wonderland Trail. When I told my son about it, he asked if we could do it together this summer so we started to research what was involved.

One of the hardest things is getting a permit, which is very competitive. The permit requests were due on April 1st (we submitted it weeks ago). Starting April 2, the park service does a lottery to fill the permit requests. So now we're just waiting to hear if we will get a permit.
