Getting More Normal

Beginning to Migrate Sites Again

John Starr
Drupal Developer
University of Minnesota
April 10, 2020

During the commotion of switching to working from home and the University switching to online classes, our team has been busy supporting groups needing urgent communications and our Tier 2 support people who were overwhelmed.

Now that things have calmed down, we are able to get back to working on migrating sites from Drupal 7 to 8. We have more 750 sites left to move and about a year to get the working done so it is good to be moving back to our primary responsiblities.
