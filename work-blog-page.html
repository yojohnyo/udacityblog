My Work

I have worked for the University of Minnesota for more than 20 years. I started working on their level 1 & 2 helplines and moved on to doing training then business analyst. While I was working as a buisiness analyst, I was tasked with replacing the University's content management system. We ended up choosing Drupal. During the implementation, I became heaviliy involved with rolling it out and learned quite a bit along the way. In the last 5 years, I've managed our Drupal contracts and done quite a bit of back end development. Most recently I developed a complex set of migrations from Drupal 7 (using panelizer) to Drupal 8 using Layout Builder. I'm hoping to expand my role and opportunities by taking on front end development as well.
