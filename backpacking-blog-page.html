Why Backpacking?

I first went backpacking in the early 90's while I was in college and fell in love with it right away. When I reached an impass in my career in 1998 and wasn't sure what I should do with the next chapter of my life, I decided to take a year off from work and thru hike the whole 2153 miles of the Appalachian Trail. I is an experience which has stuck with me ever since and I dream of returning for another thru hike when I retire. In the mean time, I have to content myself with backpacking every year or two providing that time and my health permit me to.
