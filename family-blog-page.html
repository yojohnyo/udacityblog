About My Family

My name is John Starr and I live in Minnesota with my wife, 2 kids and a dog. When we are not traveling (which unfortunately is too often) we spend our time taking the dog for walks, playing board games among ourselves and / or friends and working around the house. I am a life long competitive swimmer (I started training when I was about 8 years old) and if COVID-19 lets up in time, I hope to do some open water swims this year.
